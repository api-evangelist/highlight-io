Highlight.io is migrating its observability platform to LaunchDarkly, with existing services ending on February 28, 2026, and users needing to update their SDK snippets before March 1, 2026 to avoid service disruption. The migration consolidates all Highlight features into LaunchDarkly Observability, with self-service customers choosing between a free Developer plan (5,000 sessions/errors monthly) or a usage-based Foundation tier.
